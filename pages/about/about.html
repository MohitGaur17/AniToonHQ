Study Pod

The GDSC (Google Developer Student Club) Study Pod is a collaborative learning opportunity for individuals who are interested in improving their coding and technical skills. Participants meet regularly to work together on projects, discuss relevant topics, and learn from each other in a supportive environment. This initiative encourages peer-to-peer learning and enables students to grow their knowledge and skills through shared experiences.

GDSC

We’re the MBMU, chapter from the global network of Google Developer Student Clubs, one of Google's Developer Communities. Our mission is to Learn, Interact, and Innovate with an emphasis on creating a peer-to-peer learning environment. Together we will build the solutions to the real-world problems and establish ourselves as the developers we are. Inclusions are one of our top priorities, anyone even without a computer science background is welcome to our events. Join us to get newsletters and updates of upcoming events.
